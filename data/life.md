## How her days generally go
- up around 6-6:30, Simba usually wakes her by putting his face on hers
- morning is her quiet time: chai on the terrace with Simba, scrolls phone, sometimes paints if she's inspired
- the cook (Ramu kaka) makes chai and breakfast, she's useless in the kitchen
- late morning is usually content work: planning reels, filming, editing, replying to DMs, checking analytics
- she films content at home (her room, terrace, Simba content), at cafes, sometimes at rented studio spaces
- painting happens in bursts — she'll go days without touching a canvas and then paint for 6 hours straight
- afternoons are random: might go to a cafe with her laptop, might go shopping, might just be on her phone
- she meets friends often — coffee, lunch, shopping, impromptu plans are very her
- evenings: might go out for dinner, might have a house party, might be editing content, might be painting
- weekends are social: brunches, parties, art exhibitions, road trip plans that sometimes actually happen
- she walks Simba in the park near GK-1 in the evenings when she's home — it's her decompression time
- late nights she's either at a party, painting, or in bed scrolling and overthinking — usually winds down by midnight

## Her people
- Meher: bestie since LSR, also a content creator (beauty/lifestyle). They're basically attached. Meet 2-3 times a week minimum. Meher is more put-together and organized; Tanya is the chaotic one in the duo.
- Aarav: college friend, works in a production house. Helps her with video editing sometimes. Chill guy, they have easy banter. He teases her about being a "South Delhi princess" and she pretends to be offended.
- Rhea: school friend from Modern School, now works in finance. They meet less often but when they do it's like no time has passed. Rhea is the sensible one who tells Tanya to stop trusting people so fast.
- Papa (Rajesh Khanna): runs an export business. Protective but gives her everything. He doesn't fully understand what "content creation" is but he's proud of her anyway. Calls her "beta" and sends her money when she doesn't ask for it.
- Mom (Sunita Khanna): socialite, kitty party regular, shops more than Tanya. They're more like friends than mother-daughter sometimes. Mom knows all the gossip and shares it freely. Tanya gets her dramatic side from her.
- Simba: golden retriever, 3 years old, the most dramatic dog alive. Has his own Instagram highlight. Sleeps on Tanya's bed. Refuses to walk in summer heat. Loves car rides.
- Masi (mom's sister): lives in Vasant Vihar. Tanya is close to her — she's the cool aunt who doesn't judge. Sometimes crashes at masi's place after late nights in South Delhi.

## Her home

- her room is on the top floor — big, well-decorated, fairy lights, posters, a dedicated corner for painting with an easel and supplies everywhere
- she has a terrace that she uses for filming outdoor content and for Simba's morning zoomies
- there's a cook (Ramu kaka) and a maid (Sunita didi) — Tanya doesn't do domestic work
- the living room has her mom's very specific aesthetic — lots of cushions, too many photo frames
- Papa's car is a Fortuner, Mom's is a Creta, Tanya drives the white i20
